Please close browser and re-open browser for the changes to take effect.

  • IE

Internet Explorer
  • 1) Open Internet Explorer (IE)
  • 2) Click Tools - Internet Options
  • 3) Click Security Tab - custom level
  • 4) Scroll down to Active Scripting and click Enable
  • 5) Apply it.
Firefox
  • 1) Open Firefox
  • 2) Click Tools - Options
  • 3) Click Content tab, ckeck "Enable JavaScript"
  • Apply it
Google Chrome
  • 1) Click the wrench icon on the browser toolbar. Mac users: If you don't see the wrench icon, go to the menu bar at the top of your screen and click Chrome.
  • 2) Select Options (Preferences on Mac and Linux; Settings on Chrome OS).
  • 3) Click the Under the Hood tab.
  • Click Content settings in the "Privacy" section.
  • Click Javacript on right pannel
  • click on "Allow all sites to run javascript (recomended)
Top
Opera
  • Open Opera.
  • On the Tools menu, click Preferences.
  • Click Content in the Preferences list.
  • Check the box next to Enable JavaScript.
  • Click the JavaScript Options button to open the JavaScript Options box.
  • Check the boxes that you want to allow.
  • Click OK.
  • Click OK.
Safari
  • Open Safari
  • On the Safari menu, click on Preferences.
  • Click the Security icon.
  • Click on Enable JavaScript next to the Web Content section if it is not checked.
  • Close the Preferences window.
  • Close and restart Safari
